About NyayAssist

NyayAssist is an AI-powered legal expert system created as part of an Artificial Intelligence project. It is designed to help users understand legal situations by analyzing inputs using structured rule-based reasoning.

What NyayAssist Does

  • Provides rule-based legal decision assistance
  • Explains reasoning behind outcomes
  • Gives actionable recommendations for legal situations
  • Supports multiple legal domains

Supported Legal Domains

  • Tenant–Landlord Disputes
  • Consumer Rights
  • Contract Disputes
  • Traffic Violations
  • Cyber Fraud / Online Crime
  • Employment / Workplace Issues

How It Works

NyayAssist uses Prolog as its reasoning engine to evaluate legal rules and Python Flask to provide a smooth and interactive web interface. User inputs are converted to Prolog facts which are checked against predefined legal rules.

Disclaimer

NyayAssist is an academic project created for learning and demonstration purposes only. It provides simplified legal guidance based on predefined rules and should not be considered formal legal advice.
